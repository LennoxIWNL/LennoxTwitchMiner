## üìñ How to Use

1. **Launch the app** from your desktop shortcut or start menu
2. **Configure Settings:**
   - Add your Discord webhook URL (for notifications)
   - Enter your Twitch username
   - Choose light or dark theme
3. **Add Streamers:**
   - Paste Twitch channel URLs or usernames
   - Profile pictures load automatically
4. **Start Mining:**
   - Click "START MINING"
   - The app will automatically watch streams and collect points
   - Get real-time Discord notifications

### Setting up Discord Notifications

1. Go to your Discord server settings
2. Navigate to **Integrations ‚Üí Webhooks**
3. Click **"New Webhook"**
4. Copy the webhook URL
5. Paste it in the Settings tab of the app

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### App won't start
**Solution:** Make sure your antivirus isn't blocking it. Try running as administrator.

### No Discord notifications
**Solution:** Double-check your webhook URL in settings. Make sure the Discord bot has permission to post in the channel.

### App crashes or freezes
**Solution:** Use the "Clear All Data" button in Settings to reset, then restart the app.

### Points not collecting
**Solution:** Ensure the streamers are actually live and have channel points enabled.
